due to advances in medical technology
some people born without hearing now gain the sense later in life
among their surprises is the fact that the sun
combusts in silence
there is no constant hum above us
like a refrigerator or fluorescent bulb
as it broils and beats down on asphalt,
it should spit, hiss, and roar
it could babble or burble
as it dapples the forest floor
its perfect light and heat so distant
and yet close enough to bathe in
